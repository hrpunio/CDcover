The idea to record the performance output of the staff members from
Department of~Organ, Harpsichord, Accordion and Guitar of~Stanis³aw

music. One can find excellent examples of that on this disc.
 \looseness-1

A~dominant of the disc includes the most recent achievements of Polish
composers and the \ostyle{400} anniversary of birth of~Johannes Hevelius, noted
astronomer, marking the year \ostyle{2011}. 
This jubilee offered the opportunity
for creation of \textit{Four Hevelian Dances\/}---a~composition by

 \looseness-1

Another premi\'ere recorded on this disc is related to the project
{\it Gdañsk Art of 20 and 21-st-century Accordion Music

accordion repertoire through commissioning of new compositions. One of
the first works created within this project has been \textit{Caprice

An impact of the the regional tradition is also evident in the cycle

The contrastive short pieces \textit{Dz\"ek\/} and \textit{Naspik\/} are based
